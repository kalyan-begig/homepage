Founder's Note

BeGig as a platform is our homage to the countless developers who see themselves as hustlers and dreamers in the world who are looking for the right opportunity to unleash their talent. Gig working is a lifestyle choice that millions around the world have embraced and are benefiting from. With more and more impetus being laid on work life balance around the world - flexible working as a model has become the de-facto choice for millennials and Gen Z workers who are rejecting the 9-6 work culture.

Read more

The conceptualization of BeGig comes from my desire as an ambitious engineer who was always looking for a side hustle. I used to moonlight as a freelancer and always look at ways to keep upskilling and finding new opportunities. The only challenge was I did it in a very haphazard manner, relied mostly on word of mouth referral. Post marriage, my wife introduced me to gig working platforms. Instantly enamored by the call of working for global enterprises I signed up across multiple platforms but for some reason could never find the right opportunity for me. There must be a secret code! Which unfortunately I never cracked.


Frustrated, I started reading online blogs to figure out that like me there were many others who were facing the same issue. Finding the relevant gig was like striking gold!


On the other hand, at my regular role in an Indian IT Services firm, I used to daily witness the shortage of digital native talent. The whole industry was reeling under the supply demand mismatch.


The engineer in me started adding 2 and 2 together and BeGig was born. We set out to create a marketplace which focuses only on Tech gigs. But the question remained - why would we be any different than the plethora of competitors out there. And I remembered my biggest peeve - relevant opportunities. What if we created a platform that shows only relevant opportunities to the gig workers? Can we create a Tinder like app for finding the right work. Could you simply: Match - code - grow?


We pitched the idea to our parent investor, one of the largest Indian IT service providers, who was equally excited about it. While we were still in the preparation stages, Covid -19 hit, and the world went into disarray. Millions of people lost their livelihood. The irony was that the amount of work hadnt come down, the number of open positions remained the same in the IT industry. However, one of the bright sports which came out of the pandemic was, work from anywhere became a reality. This further added fuel to our fire to create this marketplace at a global scale so that people could find a way to provide for their families again.


As a result in April 2021, BeGig was born. And we set out with goals and aspirations to bring changes to the life of every digital ninja who was a dreamer and a hustler like us.
